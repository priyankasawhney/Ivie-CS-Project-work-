WHAT TO DO IF YOUR PET GETS COVID

    The virus that causes COVID-19 can spread from people to animals including pets in some situations, mostly during close contact. To protect your pet from the COVID-19 virus, don't let your dog or cat interact with people or animals outside your household. For example:

  1. Avoid dog parks or public places where many people and dogs gather.
  2. When walking your dog, make sure your dog wears a leash and keep your dog at least 6 feet (2 meters) from other people and animals.
  3. Keep cats indoors as much as possible.
  4. If a person inside the household becomes sick, isolate that person from everyone else, including pets and other animals.
    5. Pet owners and every eligible person in the household should get vaccinated.
  5. Pet owners should not allow pets to have contact with unvaccinated people outside the household, if possible.
